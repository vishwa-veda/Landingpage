Fitness

Fitness involves regular physical activity, a balanced diet, and sufficient rest. Physical fitness improves cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, enhances flexibility, and boosts overall endurance. Additionally, staying fit can improve mental well-being by reducing stress, anxiety, and depression.

Health

Health involves maintaining a balanced diet, regular exercise, adequate rest, and stress management. Good health allows individuals to lead active and productive lives. Mental health is equally important, encompassing emotional stability,and the ability to cope with life's challenges.
